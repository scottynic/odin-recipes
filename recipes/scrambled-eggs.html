Scrambled Eggs

Description

Need a delicious meal in a hurry and only have bread and eggs in the house? Make this timeless classic.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Crack two eggs into a cup and beat with a fork until well combined.
  2. Add a knob of butter to a saucepan and, when melted, pour in the egg mixture.
  3. Heat gently, stirring occasionally to 'scramble' the solidifying egg.
  4. Brown the toast nicely and butter liberally.
  5. Pour the scrambled eggs onto the toast and enjoy!
