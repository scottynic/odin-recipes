Steak and Ale Pie

Description

This is truly a pie to see you through the dark months ahead. I can't pretend it's diet-friendly fare, but it goes well with steamed greens and a little really does go a long way. As with self-control.

Ingredients

  • Stewing steak
  • Flour
  • Egg for glazing
  • Butter
  • Silverskin onions
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Red wine vinegar

Steps

  1. Braze the steak in a pan once coated with floru.
  2. Put the onions and lardons into the pan and heat under browning.
  3. Meanwhile make the pastry.
