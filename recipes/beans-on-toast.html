Beans on Toast

Description

Probably the most delicious meal in the whole world - you simply can't beat good old fashioned beans on toast.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Toast
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Bake beans in the saucepan until the juice has thickened and the beans are soft.
  2. Brown the toast and butter liberally.
  3. Pour the beans over the toast.
  4. Enjoy!
